Dancing with the Stars Trivia Quiz

For this project, I created a trivia quiz centered on *Dancing with the Stars* (DWTS). I expanded the quiz from 5 to 7 questions, updating both the content and the visual presentation.

  • Customized feedback with 15 unique responses referencing ballroom dances and iconic judges' comments.
  • Designed a DWTS-inspired theme using a dark background and white text for better readability.
  • Used JavaScript to dynamically display varied feedback for multiple quiz attempts.
