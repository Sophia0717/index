Video Production

This video demonstrates the use of Minecraft Education Edition for teaching environmental concepts.

My video project focuses on how Minecraft Education Edition can be a powerful tool for teaching environmental concepts. Using HD Movie Maker, I incorporated various effects and transitions to create an engaging and informative presentation.


The video incorporates the following features:

  • Zoom-in, zoom-out, and Ken Burns pan effects on selected images.
  • Smooth fade and dissolve transitions between clips.
  • Detailed captions synchronized with the narration.
  • Relaxing background music paired with narration.
  • A final credits slide with references included.
I carefully selected each effect to align with the theme of environmental education. The background music complements the narration, creating a cohesive and engaging experience for viewers.
