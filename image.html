My Creative Projects

Explore my design projects, including a book cover for *Little Nightmares* and a logo for BRAC's Adult Program.

Little Nightmares Book Cover

About the Project: The *Little Nightmares* book cover captures the eerie and mysterious atmosphere of the game, reflecting themes of survival and courage.

Key Design Elements:
- **Dark Cityscape:** Symbolizes the oppressive environment Six must navigate.
- **Sketch of Six:** Highlights her vulnerability and resilience.
- **Foggy Lighting:** Adds depth and mystery to the design.
- **Monstrous Figure:** Represents the threats Six faces.
- **Light Beam:** A symbol of hope and determination.
