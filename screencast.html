Screencast

A tutorial on using Padlet for education.

As an education major and aspiring guidance counselor, I created a screencast tutorial to demonstrate how educators can use Padlet, a versatile and interactive learning tool. This tool is excellent for collaborative projects, and my goal was to showcase its potential to educators and students unfamiliar with its features.

Creating the Screencast
To begin, I outlined a detailed script covering every step, including logging in, customizing a Padlet board, adding posts, and sharing the board with collaborators. I practiced multiple times to ensure a smooth and clear delivery while navigating the platform.

Recording and Editing
I used Screencastify to record the tutorial in one take, walking viewers through each step while narrating. Afterward, I reviewed and edited the video within Screencastify to remove unnecessary pauses and ensure a seamless flow. For final adjustments, I used iMovie to enhance the video quality before uploading it to YouTube.

Highlights
In the tutorial, I demonstrated various Padlet formats, customization options, and collaborative features, providing tips and best practices to maximize its educational value.
